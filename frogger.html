Frogger......

Enemies

These are the enemies that are constantly roaming around to kill the player. Make sure to not encounter them

River

These are rivers. Make sure to avoid them. The player doesnot know how to swim

Logs

These are logs floating on the river. You can cross the river by stepping on these logs.

Destination

This represents the destination. You have to cross all the blockades you have in your way and step on top of this to win the game

Dead Frog

This represents the dead frog. This will be your player's state when it encounters an enemy or it falls into the river
